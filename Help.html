Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a TechRoute account?

To create a TechRoute account, simply click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and verify your email to get started.

How can I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

How do I change my career roadmap?

You can change your career roadmap by going to your dashboard, selecting "My Roadmaps," and then choosing "Change Roadmap." From there, you can select a new career path that interests you.

How do I enable or disable reminders?

To manage reminders, go to your profile settings and select the "Notifications" tab. From there, you can toggle reminders on or off and customize when you receive them.

How can I contact TechRoute support?

You can contact our support team by calling 8142763713, emailing support@techroute.com, or using the contact form on this page. Our support hours are Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
